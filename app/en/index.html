FLUENTRUSSIA is a Moscow and Chelyabinsk-based Ed Tech company that is passionate about life-long learning.  FR is Russia's leading provider of technology-based language training, after school programs for kids and teens and continuing and professional education courses for adults. We are committed to delivering high quality, supplementary education to all Russians-in-person and live online-- no matter where they live. We put great instructors at the heart of the classroom and e-learning experience because we know this combination delivers life-changing impact. Whether FR serves students directly, or through partnerships with companies, governments and schools, in the classroom or live online, the goal is always the same: To improve the lives of all Russians through education.

What we do

FLUENTRUSSIA is a Moscow and Chelyabinsk-based Ed Tech company that is passionate about life-long learning.  FR is Russia's leading provider of technology-based language training, after school programs for kids and teens and continuing and professional education courses for adults. We are committed to delivering high quality, supplementary education to all Russians-in-person and live online-- no matter where they live. We put great instructors at the heart of the classroom and e-learning experience because we know this combination delivers life-changing impact. Whether FR serves students directly, or through partnerships with companies, governments and schools, in the classroom or live online, the goal is always the same: To improve the lives of all Russians through education.

Программирование

В современном мире программирование перестает быть отдельной профессией и становится одной из важных компетенций, которыми должен обладать практически любой человек.

Наши курсы обучения созданы для детей от 10-ти лет и взрослых, и построены на практико-ориентированном подходе.

В ходе обучения на всех его этапах Вы будете создавать собственный проект – предполагаемый осязаемый конечный результат.

Курсы обучения ведут учителя физико-математической школы с углубленным изучением информатики №31 г. Челябинска, входящей в тройку сильнейших школ России по уровню конкурентоспособности выпускников (по данным Рейтингового агентства «RaEx»).

Full-time format

Despite our reputation for digital transformation and the virtualization of language and skills training, we still strongly believe in the value of face-to-face training. In fact, FLUENTRUSSIA is the only training organization in Russia that can be a truly national partner, delivering face-to-face training in more than thirty cities.

DISTANCE COURSES

With FLUENTRUSSIA's virtual training services students gain access to Russia and the world's best trainers - wherever you are in the world. Our Virtual services are available via through our .beconnected embedded platform, Skype, GoToMeeting, Web Chat or even by telephone. FLUENTRUSSIA has been a pioneer and leader in virtual instructor-led training for more than 10 years, winning multiple awards, and driving ground-breaking innovation with several unique service.

DIGITAL

FR Digital is our online, interactive, self-study service, where students learn at their own pace without instruction or support from a trainer or tutor. We offer the world's most comprehensive digital courseware with exciting online course Moreover, both our core curricula and our mobile applications and micro learning can be customized with specialized skills and client-specific materials, so there's no limit to where students can go.
